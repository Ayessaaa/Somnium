Forest adventure trying to find a treasure chest
I woke up to the sound of birds chirping outside my window. Today was the day—Jack and I were going to find the treasure chest hidden deep in the forest. The old wizard’s story echoed in my head: “Only the brave and kind-hearted will succeed.” We grabbed our backpacks and set off. The forest was alive with rustling leaves and sunlight peeking through the trees. My heart raced when we found the first clue etched into an old tree: “Follow the path where flowers glow.” As we walked, glowing blue flowers lit the way. We crossed a wobbly bridge, dodged a playful squirrel, and finally reached a clearing. There it was—the treasure chest! Jack and I pushed open the lid, and inside, we found shiny coins and a note: “The true treasure is your courage and friendship.” I smiled at Jack. This was the best adventure ever.
